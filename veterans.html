Supporting Our Veterans

A Special Commitment to Veterans

At our practice, we are honored to support those who have bravely served our country. Dr. Neal is dedicated to providing exceptional dental care to our veterans, ensuring they receive the respect and attention they deserve.

In addition to offering complimentary dental services around Veterans Day, Dr. Neal also provides discounts and often waives fees for veterans on a case-by-case basis throughout the year. This initiative is our way of showing ongoing gratitude and giving back to those who have sacrificed so much for our freedom.

Complimentary Services and Discounts

  • Veteran's Day: Enjoy free dental services as our special thank you.
  • Year-Round Support: Discounts and fee waivers are available on a case-by-case basis throughout the year.

We extend our deepest thanks to all veterans for your courage and dedication. We are committed to supporting you with compassionate care and are honored to serve you. For more information or to discuss how we can assist you, please reach out to us.
