Meet Dr. Neal

DR. NEAL earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology with a minor in Chemistry from the University of Lowell in Massachusetts. He then completed his Doctorate of Dental Medicine (DMD) at Tufts University School of Dental Medicine in Boston in 1983. Shortly after, he and his wife Eileen purchased the dental practice and home at 190 South Main Street in Wolfeboro. They quickly became integral members of the community, raising four children and coaching numerous local sports teams.

Dr. Neal is a member of the American Dental Association and has served as president of the Lakes Region Dental Society, both in the past and again for the 2024-2025 term. He is a four-time Past Master of the Morning Star Lodge #17 and a former president of the Rotary Club. He remains actively involved in both organizations, frequently participating in their fundraising efforts. Additionally, he has served on the Board of Directors for both Huggins Hospital and the Wolfeboro Area Recreation Association.

Outside of his professional and community commitments, Dr. Neal enjoys spending time with his family and friends pursuing various hobbies. His interests include golfing, yoga, spinning, attending concerts, hiking with his family dogs, and improving his guitar skills with friends. He is also currently learning to play the alto saxophone. Meeting Dr. Neal, you'll quickly see his deep passion for dentistry and his infectious enthusiasm for life.
