Dental Emergency?

Dr. Neal is available to his established patients for urgent needs most evenings and weekends. During office closures for vacation, he arranges for coverage with a trusted local dentist to ensure his patients receive care. For dental emergencies outside regular office hours, please call the office for guidance.

About Us

At Dr. Neal's Dental Practice, your health and well-being are our top priorities. We are excited to welcome you to a practice where exceptional care and personalized attention define every visit. Dr. Neal and our dedicated team are here to ensure your experience is as comfortable and effective as possible.

Dr. Neal is a highly skilled general dentist with a commitment to providing comprehensive care tailored to your unique needs. Our practice is built on principles of respect, trust, and personalized attention. We believe that your smile plays a crucial role in your overall health, and we are dedicated to helping you maintain it.

Our Philosophy

At Dr. Neal's Dental Practice, our philosophy is simple: we treat our patients the way we would like to be treated. We prioritize your comfort, safety, and satisfaction by taking the time to listen to your concerns, answer your questions, and create a personalized treatment plan that aligns with your goals. We believe that respectful, compassionate care is the cornerstone of a positive dental experience.
